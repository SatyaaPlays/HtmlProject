KFC Stumps

Zings Stumps, which is a joint venture between Fox Sports and Cricket Australia, will be in use in every BBL game this summer, starting with Friday's Melbourne derby. And while they will add another level of excitement for fans watching on television and at the ground, the new technology could, in future, help umpires make more accurate decisions during games.

At this stage the lighting is there to add to spectator enjoyment of the game and Zings will not be used for decision making until after further testing.
